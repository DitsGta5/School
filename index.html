About Our School

Secondary School No. 190 was founded in 1985 and is located in Yalangach massif, 10, Chilanzar district, Tashkent.

We teach more than 2200 students from grades 1 to 11. The school is famous for its strong academic results, modern laboratories and victories in national olympiads.

  • 2200+ students
  • 150+ qualified teachers
  • Computer & science laboratories
  • Winners of National Olympiads 2023–2025
  • Trilingual education: Uzbek · Russian · English
